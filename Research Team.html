LAVIS is a first-of-its-kind Multi-function Surgical Forceps designed by Dr. Christos Panotopoulos from Greece, a Neurosurgeon and NeuroScientist, renowned for contributions towards many medical related scientific inventions.

LAVIS means ‘Forceps’ in Greek language. The device is a innovation based on the need to enhance the speed of surgery and to reduce the strain on the surgeons/ medical professionals.

Surgeons / Medical professionals use Bipolar Forceps to conduct surgical interventions.

Current Bipolars perform only the function of coagulation / cutting.

Each action is cumbersome and increases time of surgery.

More medical professionals are required to perform additional functions of aspiration and irrigation, creating space constraints and limiting the surgeons movements.
